Books that altered my brain chemistry

Before The Coffee Gets Cold Series

Toshikazu Kawaguchi

The 'Before the Coffee Gets Cold' series revolves around a small, mysterious café in Tokyo named Funiculi Funicula. The café is known for serving a special type of coffee that allows patrons to travel back in time and revisit specific moments in their lives. However, there are certain rules and limitations to this time-traveling coffee. The story atracted me, not jsu because of the concept, but how the concept caputrues the stories of people, and their missed chances, and feelings of regret.

Someone Who Will Love You In Your Damaged Glory

Raphael Bob-Waksberg

"Someone Who Will Love You in Your Damaged Glory" is a collection of short stories written by Raphael Bob-Waksberg, the creator of the acclaimed animated series "BoJack Horseman." Each story in the collection offers a glimpse into the complexities of human relationships and the intricacies of modern life. The narratives are characterized by a unique blend of humor, wit, and a profound exploration of human emotions.
